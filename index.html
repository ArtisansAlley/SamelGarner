About Me

If you would have told me a year ago that I would pray for hundreds of people and see 150+ of them healed I would have believed you...

If you would have told me that in this season God would uproot hurt from 20 yrs of growing up in the South, I might have believed you...

But If you would have told me that I would be a part of a congregation that would celebrate the "Crazy Dreams" to change the world that God has put in my heart... That would have taken slightly more faith from me.

This year has simultaneously been one of the most difficult and one of the most glorious years of my life. I've come face to face with brokenness that I didn't know that I had. I've experienced breakthrough in witnessing Gods power move more through me. Ive wept because of relationships that have ended, and rejoiced in the victory of those I've poured into. (To mention just some of the valleys and mountain tops of this year)

In this picture is one of the men who has stood with me in the attractive and the ugly.(Pastor Gary) Me and one of the children that I have the honor of shepherding, are praying for pain that he's been experiencing in his ankle. (He got healed)

Here's where the "Crazy Dreams" part comes in. I desire to see the Bride of Christ look like Christ. I want to encourage them, release them in to their destiny, and equip them so that when the world sees you they will want Him. I see a day when healing the sick is everyday life, where addictions are broken with one loving encounter, and yes dead folks getting raised
